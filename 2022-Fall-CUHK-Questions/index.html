2022 Fall 香港中文大学 MSc 申请问题汇总

2022-03-21

本文针对本科生申请硕士项目

1 网申系统填写问题

1.1 Personal Particulars

  • Do you have a right of abode in Hong Kong? 你在香港有居留权吗？
    No
  • Do you have to apply for a student visa or entry permit issued by the Director of Immigration of HKSAR Government for entering Hong
    Kong for the purpose of education?
    你是否需要申请由香港特别行政区政府入境事务处处长签发的学生签证或进入许可来港就读，大陆人去香港要办签证，选Yes

1.2 Academic Qualifications

  • Minimum Point of GPA / Mark [*Please input "N/A" if it is not applicable]
    网上说是你所在专业的成绩最低的那个人的成绩，不知道就填 N/A
  • Means of Qualification to be Obtained
    是授课还是研究获得的资质，鉴于国内本科课程都有毕业设计论文，所以选Both没啥问题
  • Honours
    如果还没有毕业，没有荣誉称号，选NOT APPLICABLE

1.3 My Referee(s)

  • 推荐信什么时候发出？
    本人完成填写之后第二天发出，发出后不能再修改推荐人信息，会显示发送时间和截止填写日期
  • 推荐人要填写哪些问题？
    • For how long and in what capacity have you known the applicant? 你认识申请人多久，和他是什么关系
    • How would you rank the applicant in the following characteristics in comparison with other students you have taught or other employees you have worked with or supervised?
    • In your opinion, is the applicant's scholastic record or performance in the workplace, as you know it, an accurate index of his/her scholastic ability?
    • Please add any comment you think will be of assistance in assessing the applicant by flling in the textbox below OR by uploading a file in Part D. (Please write "refer to attachment" in the below textbox if you opt to upload a file.) 可以上传pdf推荐信
    • Would you recommend the applicant to pursue postgraduate studies for the programme applied for? 单选：非常推荐、推荐、不推荐之类的

2 邮寄纸质材料问题

2.1 本人的 Check List

  • Envelope One 此信封内容为学校官方内容，教务处盖骑缝章
    （1）Education Certificate (English)
    （2）Education Certificate (Chinese)
    （3）Academic Transcript (English)
    （4）Academic Transcript (Chinese)
    （5）Grading Scheme
  • Envelope Two 个人信息，可不盖骑缝章（教务处可能不给盖）
    （1）Transcript Submission Covering Sheet
    （2）Copy of ID Card
    （3）Personal Statement
    （4）Curriculum Vitae
    （5）Copy of Internship Certificate

俩信封写上信封编号和申请号，和Check List放到快递文件袋里边，顺丰邮寄（30元）或邮政（好像接近100元）
